Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At ShroomsBloomIdle, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification info unless provided for the leaderboard (Username). We use LocalStorage to save your game progress on your device.

2. Cookies and Ads

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We use PayPal for transactions and Supabase for leaderboard/chat data. These services have their own privacy policies regarding the data they process.

4. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via our official GitHub repository.
